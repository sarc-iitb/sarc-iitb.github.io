About Us

Student Alumni Relations Cell (SARC) is a student body run under the guidance of the Dean ACR Office, IIT Bombay. Started in 2008 we work with a vision of bringing the alumni and student communities of IITB together to create a synergy of ideas, respect, knowledge and everything that our Institute stands for.
